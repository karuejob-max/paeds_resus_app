## ERT Protocol Configuration

### Protocol Version: V2.0
- **Code Blue Activation:** Cardiac arrest, sudden deterioration, or RN discretion
- **Resus Board Update:** BEFORE start of every shift (not at)
- **8 Roles:** 7 UTLs of the shift + 1 ward nurse (Runner/Family Liaison/CCF Tracker)
- **Expert:** Consultant in-charge / Third on call
- **Reporting Window:** 6 hours post-event via WhatsApp

### Intercom Redundancy Plan
1. Primary: Hospital intercom
2. Backup 1: Mobile phone call to Pole Lead
3. Backup 2: Physical runner to nearest department

This template is the **"Level 5 Kenya"** baseline. To deploy at a new institution:
1. Import this template in the ResusGPS Institutional Portal.
2. Update: Institution name, department headcounts, ERC names.
3. Configure: WhatsApp group link, payment details, training dates.
4. Launch: Share the institution's unique sign-up URL.

**Estimated setup time: < 30 minutes.**
