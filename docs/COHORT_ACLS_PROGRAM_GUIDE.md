# Cohort ACLS/BLS Internship Training Guide

Welcome to the **Multi-Institutional Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS) & Basic Life Support (BLS) Training Program**. This 6-month program is subsidized and structured to fit your busy internship schedule, offering a flexible learning pathway and payment options.

---

## 🌟 Program Overview

* **Subsidized ACLS Program Fee:** Contact your institution or view the billing tab for your local subsidized rate (e.g. KES 15,000 for affiliated facilities, compared to the standard KES 20,000 fee).
* **BLS Program:** Certified with a Paeds Resus BLS Certificate.
* **Final Credentials:**
  1. **AHA ACLS Provider Certificate** (upon full completion of Phase 3 and course requirements).
  2. **Paeds Resus BLS Certificate**.
* **Duration:** 6 Months.

---

## 🗺️ The Three Training Phases

The training program is divided into three distinct phases to ensure cognitive preparation, virtual megacode practice, and hands-on psychomotor mastery.

```
       [ PHASE 1 ]                [ PHASE 2 ]                 [ PHASE 3 ]
   Cognitive & Prework         Online Simulations         Physical Megacodes
   • elearning.heart.org     • Evening Web Simulations   • Psychomotor Skills
   • Precourse Assessment    • Booking Calendar          • CPR & CCF Coaching
   • Video Prework           • Team Member & Leader      • Final Assessment
```

### 📘 Phase 1: Cognitive Foundation & Prework
In this initial phase, you will master the core algorithms and theories.
1. Complete the cognitive modules on the Paeds Resus platform.
2. Complete the official **AHA Video Prework** and the **ACLS Precourse Self-Assessment** on the AHA eLearning portal ([elearning.heart.org](https://elearning.heart.org)).
3. Upload your completion certificates/score reports to your provider profile.
4. *Once verified, you will be unlocked to book slots in Phase 2.*

### 🖥️ Phase 2: Online Simulations (Megacodes)
Here, you will practice virtual team dynamics and leadership roles in pediatric and adult resuscitation codes.
* **Booking Sessions:** Book scheduled evening simulation sessions directly through the training calendar on your learner dashboard.
* **Simulation Roles:**
  * You must participate in at least **3 simulations as a team member**.
  * You must participate in at least **3 simulations as a team leader**.
* **Completion Requirement:** Moving to Phase 3 is based on **proven competence**, not just the number of sessions attended. The 3/3 rule is simply the minimum practice baseline.

### 🏥 Phase 3: Physical Simulations & Final Assessment
The final phase is a high-intensity, hands-on workshop at the hospital, where you will practice with state-of-the-art simulators.
* **Psychomotor Mastery:** High-quality CPR, Chest Compression Fraction (CCF) coaching, bag-mask ventilation, defibrillation, and advanced airway management.
* **Final Megacode Assessment:** Practical evaluation of your megacode leadership.
* **Access Requirement:** You must have paid your total subsidized program fee in full to book and attend this phase.

---

## 💰 Flexible Payment Schedule

We understand that internship stipends can experience administrative delays. Therefore, we have removed all rigid payment walls for the cognitive and simulation phases:

1. **Pay at your own free time:** You can start Phase 1 and Phase 2 without making any upfront payment.
2. **Installment Ledger:** Submit payments in small chunks (e.g., KES 2,500 or KES 5,000) using **M-Pesa STK Push** directly from your Learner Dashboard whenever you have the funds.
3. **Phase 3 Gate:** Your total payment must reach the required course fee (e.g. KES 15,000) before you can select a physical megacode assessment slot.
4. **Waitlist Priority (Phase 2):** If demand for Phase 2 online simulation slots exceeds capacity, the system uses a balanced waitlist algorithm. Priority is given to those who have paid the most, but slots remain distributed to ensure unpaid learners still get simulation slots and progress.

## 🚀 How to Enroll

1. Go to the **Paeds Resus Portal** and create an individual account.
2. Complete your **Provider Profile** and select your primary affiliated facility.
3. Select your designation: **BSN Intern, Clinical Officer Intern (BSc), Diploma COI, or MOI**.
4. Go to **Courses**, select **ACLS & BLS Training**, and click **Enroll**.
5. Once your Facility Admin approves your institutional link request, you are officially in the cohort! You can monitor your dashboard progress and begin Phase 1 immediately.
