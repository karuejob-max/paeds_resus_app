# Platform Activation Playbook

## Objective

Operationalize monetization immediately by activating discoverability, packaging, and conversion pathways across modules that already exist.

## Phase 1 (0–14 days): Turn on monetization visibility

### 1) Activation hub and routing

- Make the activation hub the default landing route.
- Expose direct navigation to:
  - Safe-Truth
  - ResusGPS
  - BLS enrollment and payment
  - Institutional onboarding and portal
  - Analytics dashboards

### 2) Core commercial offers to publish

- Individual: BLS, ACLS, PALS
- Fellowship: Bronze/Silver/Gold
- Institutional: seat-based plans with bulk discounts
- Parent: first aid/CPR essentials

### 3) Conversion instrumentation

Track at minimum:

- Landing view → module click
- Module click → enrollment start
- Enrollment start → payment intent
- Payment intent → payment success/failure

## Phase 2 (15–45 days): Increase ARPU and retention

### 1) Guided upsells

- Offer next best product at completion milestones.
- Trigger fellowship recommendations for high-engagement learners.

### 2) Institutional monetization

- Route every institutional inquiry through onboarding + quotation workflow.
- Prioritize procurement-friendly payment options (bank transfer + invoicing).

### 3) Recurring revenue layer

- Add recertification reminders and renewal prompts.
- Add verification/reporting bundles for institutional buyers.

## Phase 3 (46–90 days): Expand platform revenue surface

### 1) Marketplace monetization

- Launch premium first-party add-ons.
- Introduce third-party partner add-ons with revenue sharing.

### 2) Region/currency expansion

- Use supported payment methods and currency conversion to test non-KES growth corridors.

### 3) Executive dashboard cadence

- Weekly revenue review.
- Monthly product/funnel optimization cycle.

## Decision Log Template

For every monetization change, append a log entry:

- Date
- Decision owner
- Change made
- KPI expected to improve
- Risk
- Rollback plan

## KPI starter pack

- Conversion rate (activation hub to paid)
- Payment success rate (by method)
- ARPU/ARPA
- Institutional close rate
- Renewal rate
- Marketplace GMV
