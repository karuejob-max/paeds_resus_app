# Paeds Resus - Strategic Vision & Operational Plan 2026-2031

## Executive Summary

Paeds Resus is a fully autonomous pediatric resuscitation training and incident management platform designed to eliminate preventable child deaths across Africa and beyond. This document outlines the comprehensive strategic vision, operational roadmap, and detailed execution plan through 2031.

**Mission:** No child should die from preventable causes.

**Vision 2031:** Paeds Resus is the global standard for pediatric training and incident management, deployed across 54 African countries with 5,000 hospitals, 500,000 trained healthcare workers, and 200,000+ lives saved.

**Operating Model:** Fully autonomous, continuously improving (kaizen), financially sustainable, and mission-focused.
